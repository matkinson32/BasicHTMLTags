How to make Sour Candy Grapes

Tastes just like sour patch kids!

  • Wash Grapes
  • Freeze Grapes for 2 hours
  • Take them out of freezer
  • Squeeze a fresh lemon over grapes
  • Make sure to get each grape with lemon juice or put in a bowl and shake them up
  • Enjoy!

If you really want to get fun with the grapes you can also put them in jello powder and shake them around!

Just to give you some inspiration!
